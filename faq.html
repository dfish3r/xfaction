FAQ


Philosophical
Why does this site exist? We wanted a way to coordinate conversation and play times with characters on other realms and factions. The simplest way for us to do so was to develop a real time text based web site.
Why not use ventrilo, teamspeak, etc? We wanted a tool that provided the lowest possible barrier to entry to encourage participation. Text is lower bandwidth, doesn't require that you be "on" it all the time to see the messages and many players appreciate the anonymity that text communication provides.

World of Warcraft Related
Do I use my World of Warcraft password? No, never give Xfaction any information regarding your World of Warcraft account. In fact, we recommend you never give out any personal information in either Xfaction chat rooms or in 1-to-1 chats with other users.
Does this application interact with World of Warcraft? No, Xfaction does not interact with either your World of Warcraft client or the World of Warcraft servers.
Usage
I don't like typing my character name everytime I login. Set your browser to allow cookies from xfaction.net. The character name and realm from your last successful login will be used the next time you visit www.xfaction.net.
Do I have to use your web interface? No, Xfaction is powered by Jabber. You can use any jabber capable instant messaging client you want. Clients known to work:
  • Pidgin (Windows/Linux/Mac)
  • Psi (Windows/Linux/Max)
  • Gajim (Windows/Linux)
  • Adium (Mac)
  • iChat (Mac)
  • JBother (Java)
If you are using a client other than those listed above, please let us know.
See our help instructions for how to setup some of the clients listed above.
How do you play and keep up with chat? There is no expectation that you would play and chat at the same time. In fact, most people only pay attention in-between battlegrounds. However, if you are a good multi-tasker, here are some options:
  • Duel Monitors; ok, that one was obvious.
  • Alt-Tab while you are playing; takes some coordination and requires you to run WOW in windowed mode.
  • Afloat; keep your chat window on top. (Mac only)
  • There are several ‘always on top’ applications for windows, but I have not used them. Please contact us if you have found something that works.
I'm having problems connecting or staying connected. See our troubleshooting guide
Can I join more than one chat room? Yes, users are allowed to join up to 10 chat rooms at the same time.
I don't like the public chat rooms. Xfaction can also be used to chat directly with other users in a 1-to-1 chat. In addition, you can also create your own private rooms and configure them however you like.
