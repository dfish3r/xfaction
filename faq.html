FAQ


Why does this site exist? We found our community of players was fragmented due to the difficulty of communicating who is online and what they doing in game. This site was developed to facilitate communication across realms and factions for the express purpose of improving each individuals game experience.
Why not use ventrilo, teamspeak, etc? We wanted a tool that provided the lowest possible barrier to entry to encourage participation. In addition, many players appreciate the anonymity that text communication provides.
What technologies does this site use? Xfaction leverages Apache, Jabber, and JWChat.
